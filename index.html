Neurologists, who specialize in neurology, are trained to evaluate and treat a wide range of neurological conditions. They employ various diagnostic techniques, including clinical examinations, imaging studies (such as magnetic resonance imaging or MRI), electroencephalography (EEG), and nerve conduction studies to assess and diagnose neurological disorders.
